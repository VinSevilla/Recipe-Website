Honey Garlic Chicken Breast

Description

Sliced chicken breast marinated in a honey and soysauce conenetrate

Steps

  1. Split chicken breast in half so 2 breasts makes 4 pieces
  2. Dust chicken with flour (gives the chicken a slight crust so the sauce clings to it better!)
  3. Sear chicken, add butter, garlic, vinegar, soy sauce, honey.
  4. Simmer until the sauce thickens. Turn chicken to coat in the amazing honey garlic sauce.
